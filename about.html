Our Ethics

What we do is as important as how we do it

Privacy is a priority

With a lot of data comes a lot of responsibility. We work hard to make sure that your information remains anonymous and secure. Your trust is important to us, so we will never share your personally identifable health information.

The Viome Way

There's an old way to do business in health care.
And then there's the Viome way.

There's a lot of science and math behind Amino. We're working with the best and brightest to make sure our analyses are conducted with rigor and integrity. Our goal is to responsibly present facts in a way that can help anyone make a decision. We use best practices in our analyses to present insights that are accurate and unbiased.

As with any analysis, there are some gaps in what we can measure. When we think something might be misinterpreted, we'll be transparent and let you know up front. We also welcome helpful feedback from the community!

Security

We do ask for personal information as you continue to use our service. That data is kept separate from the health care data in our database.

For more information about our privacy practices, see our Privacy Policy.
