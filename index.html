Why does Viome exist?

With trillions of dollars being spent on healthcare we continue to see our loved ones suffering from chronic conditions because our healthcare system is busy treating symptoms not figuring out the root cause.

What is Viome's approach?

Our body is a dynamic and ever changing ecosystem and monitoring it at a molecular level on a regular basis and providing actionable insight based on this high resolution data will enable us to optimize our health.
