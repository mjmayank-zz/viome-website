VIOME ACTIVE

Viome is a wellness service that uses your diet, activity, environment, medical and genomic data to guide you to better health!

How it works
Collect

We'll send you a kit that contains all the materials and instructions necessary for you to collect your samples in the comfort of your own home. Mail it back to us in a prepaid envelope. It's as simple as that.

Analyze

We process your samples in our state of the art facilities to help generate a picture of your body at a molecular level. We combine that with our doctor-trained AI engine to generate actionable diet, exercise, and supplement recommendations that you can follow on a daily basis
